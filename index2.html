The Public Safety Bureau's Criminal Investigation Division

Inspector Tsunemori AkaneLead character
We meet this rookie detective on her first day in the job and follow her as she tries to get to know the members of the team and struggles with the morality of day-to-day crime-fighting under the Sibyl system's authority.


Kogami ShinyaTroubled hero
An enforcer under Akane's supervision. His quick-thinking often outsmarts the criminals and saves the team but there is one criminal mastermind he still hasn't caught up with. Can be reckless at times but his heart's in the right place. Likes reading old books.


Nobuchika GinozaInspector
This guy comes across very serious and can be blunt and critical at times but he has his reasons. He does things by the book and tries to avoid unnecessary risks. Protective and reliable, if not that much fun at a party...


Karanomori ShionData genius
Seductive and smart, Shion does not shy away from office romances. She's a quick-thinker in a crisis and the team's 'go to' member when they need to find information or hack into a security systems.


Kunizuka YayoiRockstar-turned enforcer
Once a promising musician, Yayoi's psycho-pass got clouded and she was recruited to the team by Kogami. She is known for being level-headed but also shows a caring side every once in a while.


Shusei KagariJovial enforcer
Kagari is another enforcer under Akane's supervision. Fiercely loyal to the other team members, and tries his best to do right by them. He has a keen sense of humour and often tries to lighten everyone's mood.


Tomomi MasaokaFather figure/enforcer
Masaoka is often called by his nickname 'Pops', a reference to his being older than the other team members and quite like a father figure. He has been working in this job a long time and his detective's intuition is rarely wrong.


And the man who fights against them

Makishima ShogoVillain of the piece
A man with a plan to destroy the existing Sibyl system and start a social revolution. Has great hair. Also likes old books.
