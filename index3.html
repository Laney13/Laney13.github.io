Dominators: the eyes of Sibyl

Dominators

The guns carried by all inspectors and enforcers are called Dominators, often referred to as the 'eyes of Sibyl'. They are used to check a suspect's criminal coefficient- the measure that decides if they are guilty of a crime or not- and the gun then adapts itself to the level of crime coefficient to authorise and administer justice at the appropriate level.


Dominator Levels

If a suspect's crime coefficient is less than 100, the dominators will not work on them. If it is 100+ but less than 300 and the dominator mode switches to 'non-lethal paralyser' which renders a suspect immobile so they can be detained.

300+ and the dominator mode switches to 'lethal eliminator', killing the suspect in a gruesome way (watch the series because I don't know how to describe it... eek!).

If the target is a machine or armoured, the mode engaged is Destroy Decomposer- bad robot :P

There is one more 'mode', as it could be called, but it come in the form of an upgraded gun which can take out multiple target and has a bipod to steady it for the user.
